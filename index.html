About Me

Hi, I’m Manjinder. I have an Associate of Arts in Psychology and focus on helping people understand stress and develop healthier daily routines.

My coaching is educational and lifestyle-based. I do not provide therapy or medical treatment, but I guide clients to build practical habits that support emotional balance, focus, and wellbeing.

Who This Is For

  • People feeling stressed or overwhelmed
  • Students or working adults seeking balance
  • Anyone wanting simple, effective routines
  • Those looking for non-clinical support and guidance

How I Work

  1. Free initial consultation to understand your needs
  2. Identifying stress triggers and challenges
  3. Building practical, daily routines for balance
  4. Ongoing guidance and check-ins to stay on track
